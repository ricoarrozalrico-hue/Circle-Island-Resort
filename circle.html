Attraction

Circle Island Resort is a fun-filled water retreat located in Molino III, Bacoor City. With spacious pools, water slides, and tropical-themed cottages, it’s the perfect venue for family outings, school trips, and group celebrations.

Accommodation

Cottages

Bamboo and native-style cottages for groups of 5–15 pax. Affordable rates starting at ₱400.

Hotel Rooms

Air-conditioned family and standard rooms available for overnight stays, with free parking and room service.

Activities

  • Day & Night Swimming (with water slides)
  • Birthday parties, reunions, and gatherings
  • On-site dining and catering services

Amenities

Pools

Kiddie and adult pools with slides

Food

Restaurant and bar & grill available inside the resort

Parking

Secure and free parking space for guests

Accessibility

Located along Daang Hari Road, Molino III, Bacoor Cavite. Easily accessible by car or public transport, just a short drive from Las Piñas and Alabang. Nearby malls include SM Molino and Vista Mall.
